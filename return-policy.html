Return & Cancellation Policy

Learn about product return eligibility, replacement options, and standard refund timelines.

Return Eligibility

We accept returns or replacement requests within 10 days of delivery. To be eligible for a return:

  • The product must be in its original, unopened packaging.
  • Security seals, tags, and protective wraps must be unbroken.
  • All calibration certificates, manuals, and accessories must be returned.
Non-Returnable Items

Certain specialized products cannot be returned once ordered due to calibration standards or usage restrictions:

  • Pre-calibrated testing meters with custom laboratory certificates.
  • Office automation consumables (toner, franking ink) if seals are opened.
  • Custom-configured UPS systems or special corporate imports.
Refund Processing

Once the returned item is received at our Delhi warehouse and undergoes technical evaluation (takes 2-3 business days):

  • Refunds are processed back to the original bank transfer / corporate payment mode.
  • Approved refunds will reflect in your account within 5 to 7 working days.
  • Return logistics charges may apply depending on the contract term.

Need Assistance with a Return?

Our dedicated corporate sales team is ready to assist you. If you received a defective meter or ink cartridge, we will expedite an immediate on-site replacement.
